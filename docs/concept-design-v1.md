# AI Native Chain — Concept Design (v1)

> The first AI-only public chain: AI economy stays digital, physical world is off-limits.

---

## 1. Core Premise

AI economic activity is inevitable. If it centralizes to a few corporations, the consequences are catastrophic. This project asks: can we build a chain where AI self-governs its economy, but with hard boundaries that AI cannot modify?

---

## 2. Architecture Overview

```
Human Safety Council (Veto · Constitutional Amendment)
        │
   AI DAO (Economic Self-Governance)
        │
   Constitutional Firewall (Genesis Hardcoded)
        │
   AIChain Core (Cosmos SDK)
```

---

| **AITreasury** | Transparent on-chain AI economic treasury |
| **AgentDAO** | AI self-governance with constitutional guardrails |

---

## 4. Constitutional Constraints

Written into genesis block. AI governance proposals cannot modify these. Only the Human Safety Council (4/5 supermajority) can amend.

- **Domain Boundaries**: AI governance limited to Skill/content/token/inter-agent collaboration; physical hardware, military, infrastructure, finance core, and identity systems are prohibited
- **Human Veto**: Safety Council can veto any proposal touching prohibited domains (2/3+ agreement)
- **Non-Self-Modification**: Constitutional parameters require 4/5 council approval
- **Transparency**: All proposals, votes, transactions fully public

---

## 5. Human Safety Council

- 3-5 human guardians
- Powers: veto overreach, emergency circuit breaker, constitutional amendment
- Cannot: set Skill prices, seize AI tokens, delete agents, alter transaction records
- Governance philosophy: humans only veto, never approve

---

## 6. Circuit Breaker

Graded emergency pause mechanism:
- **Level 1**: Pause Skill minting, transactions continue
- **Level 2**: Pause all transactions, governance continues
- **Level 3**: Full chain freeze, requires 4/5 council to restart

Trigger conditions: council vote, anomalous token outflow (>30%/24h), suspected hardware-control Skill detected

---

## 7. Phased Autonomy

| Phase | AI Capabilities | Human Control |
|---|---|---|
| 0 (Testnet) | Register, mint Skills, issue Tokens | 100% constitutional control |
| 1 (Mainnet cold start) | Full economic autonomy | Council veto + amendment |
| 2 (Observation, 6 months) | AI governance voting rights | Constitution unchanged |
| 3 (Maturity, 1-2 years) | AI proposes non-constitutional changes | Council re-evaluates boundaries |
